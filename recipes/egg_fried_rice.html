Egg Fried Rice

Description

Simple and delicious 10-minute egg fried rice. Only uses rice, eggs, and 3 other ingredients, to provide you a full belly in a quick way.

Ingredients

  • Cooking oil
  • Egg
  • Cold rice
  • Salt and/or soy sauce
  • Green onion, optional

Steps

  1. Beat eggs well with a pinch of salt and sliced green onions. set aside.

  2. Heat a wok or a skillet over high heat. Add cooking oil, just enough to coat the pan. Pour beaten eggs into the wok and scramble.

  3. When the eggs are cooked 3/4 way, add a bit more cooking oil and the cold rice.

  4. Break down rice and mix it with the eggs.

  5. Add soy sauce around the edge of the wok, so it will burn slightly for the smoky umami flavour. Add salt to your tastes and mix everything well. Reduce heat to low if you need to.

  6. Turn off the heat, stir in green onions. Serve!
